# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for everyone, regardless of
experience level. Beginners are explicitly encouraged here.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Being kind, patient, and welcoming — especially to newcomers.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and its users.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and
other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and may ban temporarily
or permanently any contributor for behaviors they deem inappropriate.

Report concerns by opening a confidential issue or contacting the maintainers.
